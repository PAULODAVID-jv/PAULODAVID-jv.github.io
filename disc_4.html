    Research and higher education in the time of COVID-19

    ⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀The challenges faced by the academic community during the COVID 19 pandemic, both with research and with the teaching and learning of teachers and students. The essential now is the contribution of all areas mainly of researchers and scientists in the discovery of an effective vaccine against COVID19, research and diagnosis are crucial in this pandemic, but the pandemic has sucked the energy of students, researchers and scientists in the search for an urgent solution for humanity as a whole. It is a complex virus that requires a lot of study to develop some positive and effective result, requiring an infrastructure with adequate capacity and tools for progress in vaccine studies requiring many financial resources from governments and private companies. Many students do not have access to online learning and the great difficulty on their part, that is, the coronavirus pandemic has brought a major problem for society mainly for the academic community that has destabilized financially and psychologically, being a challenge to be overcome.

    Digital technology and the COVID-19 pandemic

    ⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀O Texto fala sobre a desigualdade ao acesso da Internet e suas tecnologias, por vários níveis da sociedade. O acesso da tecnologia se tornou fundamental nesse tempo de pandemia para o aprendizado dos cidadãos, sendo uma opção segura para não propagação do vírus da COVID-19, mas sempre preservando a privacidade e os direitos dos usuários no acesso à Internet. Onde governos locais e regionais devem manter o acesso a tecnologia e preservando os dados dos usuários de comunidades pobres, e que o acesso a informações e demais serviços essenciais (Educação, Entretenimento, Lazer e etc.) são fundamentais no cenário em que vivemos hoje. Com um oferecimento de serviços de Internet, Wi-Fi, garantindo transparência e ética nos padrões e normas de acesso durante a crise.
